# Client brief: Paper Crane

**Client:** Paper Crane, a stationery studio and letterpress workshop.

**What we want:** a one-page site that feels like good paper: lots of white
space, precise type, one quiet accent color.

**Sections:**

1. Hero: the name, the line "Paper, pressed properly", and a "Visit the studio"
   button.
2. What we make: three cards (wedding suites, business papers, small-run
   prints), two sentences each.
3. Workshops: a short section on the monthly letterpress evening, with a
   fictional date and a sign-up button.
4. Footer with the logo and studio hours (Tuesday to Saturday, 10:00 to 18:00).

**Brand:**

- Logo: `assets/logo.svg`. Hero and footer.
- Brand color: ink blue `#26428B`. Paper white background `#FCFBF8`.
- Type: system sans throughout, generous spacing. No webfonts.

**Hard rules:** single `index.html`, inline CSS, no external resources,
semantic HTML, alt text on every image.
