# Client brief: Harbor & Vine

**Client:** Harbor & Vine, a dockside wine bar and kitchen.

**What we want:** a one-page site that feels like the place: dark wood, low
light, the water just outside. Calm and a little formal.

**Sections:**

1. Hero: the name, the line "Wine at the water's edge", and a "Reserve a table"
   button.
2. The kitchen: three short paragraphs about the seasonal small-plates menu
   (write these in our voice: quiet confidence, no exclamation marks).
3. Hours and tides: opening hours (17:00 to 23:00, closed Mondays) presented
   next to a small note that the terrace closes at high tide.
4. Footer with the logo and a fictional pier address.

**Brand:**

- Logo: `assets/logo.svg`. Use it in the hero and the footer.
- Brand color: deep forest green `#2F5233`. That green is the house color:
  buttons, headings, accents. Off-white background `#F7F5F0`.
- Type: system serif throughout. No webfonts.

**Hard rules:** single `index.html`, inline CSS, no external resources,
semantic HTML, alt text on every image.
