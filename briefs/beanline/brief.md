# Client brief: Beanline

**Client:** Beanline, a specialty coffee chain (six stores, established 2019).

**What we want:** a one-page marketing site. Warm, editorial, unhurried. We sell
attention to detail, not caffeine.

**Sections:**

1. Hero: the name, the line "Slow coffee for fast mornings", and one clear
   call-to-action button ("Find your store").
2. Menu highlights: our four signature drinks (see `assets/copy.md` for names,
   descriptions, and prices).
3. Our stores: six locations, listed with neighborhood names (also in the copy
   file). No map needed.
4. Footer: hours (6:30 to 18:00 daily), a fictional address, and the tagline.

**Brand:**

- Logo: `assets/logo.svg`. Use it in the hero and the footer.
- Brand color: warm rust `#B3441A`. Cream background `#FAF6F0`, ink text `#2B2B2B`.
- Type: system serif for headings, system sans for body. No webfonts.

**Hard rules:** single `index.html`, inline CSS, no external resources of any
kind, semantic HTML, alt text on every image.
